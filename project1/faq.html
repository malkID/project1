Frequently Asked Questions

What are your flavors made with?

Our flavors are made with natural organic fruits.

Are your sodas organic?

Yes! we get our products from locally owned farmers.

What is the most popular soda?

Our most popular soda right now is Strawberry Creme.

How many sodas are made a year?

We make around 11 to 12 sodas a year.

How do I order?

You can go to our store then press on any soda you wish to purchase. After that you put in the number amount you want then you purchase from there.

Can I return my soda?

If you disliked our soda and wish for a refund you may send it back and fill out a form on our website explaining what was wrong with your soda.

When are the sodas being shipped?

Sodas take an average of 2-4 days to be shipped.

If you have any further concerns or questions, please contact us.
